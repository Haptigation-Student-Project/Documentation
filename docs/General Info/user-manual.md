## Introduction

### What is Haptigation?

Haptigation is an innovative **haptic navigation system** that provides turn-by-turn directions through vibration feedback. Instead of looking at a screen or listening to audio instructions, you simply feel where to go through vibrations on your wrist(s).

**Core Concept:** *"Buzz left, go left. Buzz right, go right."*

### Who is Haptigation for?

- **Cyclists and Bikers:** Navigate safely without taking your eyes off the road
- **Visually or Hearing-Impaired Individuals:** Accessible navigation through touch
- **Runners and Hikers:** Discrete guidance during outdoor activities
- **Anyone:** Who needs hands-free, eyes-free navigation

### Key Benefits

✓ **Safety First:** Keep your eyes on the road and ears open to traffic  
✓ **Intuitive:** Simple vibration patterns that are easy to understand  
✓ **Hands-Free:** No need to hold or look at your phone  
✓ **Accessible:** Alternative sensory modality for navigation  
✓ **Discrete:** Navigate without drawing attention

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Charge Your Wristband(s)

1. Connect the USB charging cable to your wristband
2. Plug the other end into a USB-C power source
3. Initial charge takes approximately 2-3 hours
4. A full charge provides approximately up to 8 hours of active navigation

### Step 2: Download the Haptigation App

1. Open the Google Play Store (Android only)
2. Search for "Haptigation"
3. Download and install the app
4. Open the app and accept the required permissions:
   - **Location Services:** Required for navigation
   - **Bluetooth:** Required to connect to wristbands

### Step 3: Pair Your Wristband(s)

1. Ensure your wristband(s) are charged and turned on
2. Open the Haptigation app
3. Tap "Settings" → "Connect Device"
4. The app will search for nearby wristbands
5. Select your wristband(s) from the list
6. Wait for the confirmation vibration
7. Your device is now paired!

**For Dual Wristband System:**
- Make sure to pair the LEFT wristband to the left position
- And the RIGHT wristband to the right position
- The app will guide you through testing each wristband

### Step 4: Wear Your Wristband(s)

**Single Wristband:**
- Wear on your preferred wrist (usually non-dominant)
- Adjust the strap for a snug but comfortable fit
- The vibration motor should be on both sides of your wrist

**Dual Wristband:**
- Wear one wristband on each wrist
- Ensure LEFT and RIGHT are correctly positioned
- Adjust both straps for comfort
- Motors should be on top of both wrists

## Using Haptigation

### Starting a Navigation Session

1. **Enter your destination** in the app
2. **Start navigation** by tapping the button
3. **Begin your journey** – the app will guide you
4. **Feel the vibrations** and respond accordingly

### During Navigation

**Following Instructions:**
- Feel a vibration on your **LEFT** wrist → Turn LEFT
- Feel a vibration on your **RIGHT** wrist → Turn RIGHT
- Feel vibrations on **BOTH** wrists → Special instruction (see patterns below)

**Checking Your Status:**
- Glance at your phone occasionally to verify route
- Battery status is shown on the app screen
- Current ETA and distance are displayed

**Adjusting the Route:**
- Tap "Alternative Routes" to see other options
- The app automatically recalculates if you miss a turn
- You'll feel a special "recalculating" pattern

### Ending Navigation

- Tap "Stop Navigation" in the app when you arrive
- Or navigation ends automatically at your destination
- Wristbands enter standby mode automatically

## Understanding Vibration Patterns

### Single Wristband Patterns (Left or Right)

| Pattern | Description | Meaning | When to Expect |
|---------|-------------|---------|----------------|
| **Very short rapid pulses** | 5 quick buzzes (100ms on, 100ms off) | Turn very soon | 200m before turn |
| **Spaced pulses** | 3 medium buzzes (150ms on, 300ms off) | Turn approaching | 50m before turn |
| **Single long vibration** | 1 long buzz (500ms) | **Turn NOW** | At turn point |

### Dual Wristband Patterns (Both Simultaneously)

| Pattern | Description | Meaning |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **U-Turn pattern** | 2 medium pulses on both (200ms on, 200ms off) | Make a U-turn |
| **Wrong direction** | 4 rapid pulses on both (150ms on, 150ms off) | You're going wrong way |
| **Recalculating** | 2 medium spaced pulses on both (250ms on, 150ms off) | Route is being recalculated |

### Tips for Pattern Recognition

- **Practice at home:** Use the "Test Vibrations" feature in the app
- **Start slow:** Begin with walking routes to learn the patterns
- **Trust the system:** The early warning system gives you time to react
- **Stay consistent:** Always wear the wristbands the same way

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues

**Problem:** Wristband won't connect to app

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
2. Restart the Haptigation app
3. Unpair and re-pair the device in app settings
4. Ensure wristband is charged

**Problem:** Connection drops during navigation

**Solutions:**
1. Keep your phone within 10 meters of wristbands
2. Ensure phone is not in ultra-power-saving mode
3. Close other Bluetooth apps that might interfere
4. Update app to latest version
5. Enable "Keep Bluetooth Active" in app settings

### Vibration Issues

**Problem:** No vibrations or weak vibrations

**Solutions:**
1. Check battery level (charge if below 20%)
2. Increase vibration intensity in app settings
3. Ensure wristband is worn correctly (motor against skin)
4. Test vibrations using app's test function
5. Check that wristband strap is snug but comfortable

**Problem:** Vibrations feel confusing

**Solutions:**
1. Practice with the pattern test mode
2. Reduce early warning distance in settings
3. Walk a test route to learn the patterns
4. Ensure LEFT and RIGHT bands are on correct wrists

### Navigation Issues

**Problem:** Getting wrong directions

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure location services are enabled and set to "Always"
2. Check that you're outdoors with clear GPS signal
3. Wait a moment for GPS to acquire accurate position
4. Verify that wristbands are on the correct wrists
5. Recalculate route in the app

**Problem:** App not tracking movement

**Solutions:**
1. Check location permissions in phone settings
2. Ensure GPS and WiFi location is enabled
3. Move to an area with better satellite visibility
4. Restart the app
5. Toggle airplane mode on and off

### Battery Issues

**Problem:** Battery drains too quickly

**Solutions:**
1. Reduce vibration intensity
3. Close app when not in use
4. Check for app updates (may include battery optimizations)
5. Ensure wristband is fully charged before long trips

**Problem:** Wristband won't charge

**Solutions:**
1. Try a different USB cable or charging adapter
2. Clean charging port gently with dry cloth
3. Ensure connections are secure
4. Try a different power source
5. Contact support if issue persists

## Care and Maintenance

### Daily Care

**Wristbands:**
- Wipe with a soft, dry cloth after each use
- Keep away from extreme temperatures
- Store in a cool, dry place when not in use
- Avoid water

**Straps:**
- Clean regularly with wet wipes
- Allow to dry completely before wearing
- Check for wear and tear periodically

### Long-Term Maintenance

**Battery Care:**
- Charge fully before long-term storage
- Recharge every 3 months if not in use
- Avoid leaving on charger for extended periods after full charge
- Battery capacity may decrease after 500+ charge cycles

**Software Updates:**
- Keep the app updated through your app store
- Update wristband firmware when prompted
- Firmware updates may improve battery life and performance

**Storage:**
- Store in original packaging or soft pouch
- Keep away from magnets and strong magnetic fields
- Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods

### When to Seek Service

Contact support if you experience:
- Persistent connection failures after troubleshooting
- Physical damage to wristband or strap
- LED indicators not functioning
- Vibration motor failure
- Water damage
- Charging port damage

## Safety Information

### Important Safety Guidelines

⚠️ **Road Safety**
- Always obey traffic laws and road signs
- Use Haptigation as an aid, not a replacement for attention
- Remain aware of your surroundings at all times
- Do not rely solely on haptic feedback in dangerous situations
- Check visual displays when safe to do so

⚠️ **Medical Considerations**
- Consult your doctor if you have circulation problems
- Do not wear if you have skin conditions on your wrists
- Remove if you experience discomfort or irritation
- Not recommended for individuals with pacemakers (consult physician)
- Vibrations may cause discomfort for those with neurological sensitivities

⚠️ **Device Safety**
- Do not attempt to open or modify the device
- Use only approved charging cables and adapters
- Keep away from water (splash-resistant, not waterproof)
- Do not expose to extreme temperatures (< 0°C or > 50°C)
- Keep away from open flames and heat sources

⚠️ **Usage Limitations**
- GPS accuracy may vary based on environment
- Not a substitute for professional navigation in critical situations
- May not function properly in tunnels or covered areas
- Requires cellular data or pre-downloaded maps

### Emergency Situations

In case of emergency:
- Prioritize your safety over navigation
- Stop in a safe location before checking your device
- Call emergency services if needed
- Do not attempt to check your phone while cycling/biking

**Haptigation is not able to check the traffic for you and solely responsible for navigation purposes only**

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Haptigation waterproof?**  
A: No

**Q: How accurate is the navigation?**  
A: Navigation accuracy depends on GPS signal quality and Google Maps mapping quality. In clear outdoor conditions, accuracy is typically within 5-10 meters, which is sufficient for turn-by-turn navigation.

**Q: Can I use Haptigation while driving a car?**  
A: Yes, there is a dedicated mode for driving in a car or on a motorcycle.

**Q: Does it work offline?**  
A: Technically, yes. Recalculating will not be possible though.

**Q: How many wristbands do I need?**  
A: One wristband is sufficient for basic navigation. Two wristbands provide a more intuitive bilateral experience and enable special instructions like U-turns. If you already own a smart watch we highly recommend using one band with our smart watch integration.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What phones are compatible?**  
A: Haptigation works only with Android 8.0+ devices with Bluetooth 4.0 or higher.

**Q: Can I pair multiple wristbands to one phone?**  
A: Yes, the dual wristband system pairs two devices. You can also switch between single and dual mode in settings.

**Q: What's the Bluetooth range?**  
A: Effective range is approximately 10 meters. Keep your phone in a pocket or bag while navigating.

**Q: Can I customize vibration patterns?**  
A: Currently, patterns are pre-designed for optimal recognition. Customization may be added in future updates.

**Q: How often should I charge the wristbands?**  
A: With regular use (1-2 hours daily), charge every 3-4 days. Battery indicator in app shows current level.

### Usage Questions

**Q: Can I use it for running?**  
A: For sure! It may help making your experience way more pleasant and intutive. We do not track any health data though, for that you will still need a smartwatch.

**Q: Will it work in other countries?**  
A: Yes, Haptigation uses Google Maps, which covers most countries worldwide. Ensure you have appropriate data connectivity or pre-downloaded maps.

**Q: Can I share routes with friends?**  
A: No, Route sharing is not possible. Please use Google Maps to use that feature instead.

**Q: What if I miss a turn?**  
A: The app automatically recalculates your route. You'll feel the "recalculating" pattern, and new directions will follow. If no route is available you will get the vibration pattern to u-turn (turn around)

**Q: Can I use it in tunnels?**  
A: GPS signal may be lost in tunnels. The app will resume guidance when signal is restored.
